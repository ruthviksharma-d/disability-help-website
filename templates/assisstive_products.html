How to Apply for Assistive Devices

1

Medical Assessment

Get assessed by a certified medical professional to determine your specific needs and eligibility for assistive devices.

2

Document Preparation

Gather required documents including disability certificate, income proof, medical prescription, and identity proof.

3

Application Submission

Submit your application through the ADIP scheme portal, district collector office, or designated implementing agencies.

4

Verification & Approval

Your application will be verified and processed. Approval typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the device type.

5

Device Fitting & Training

Receive your device with proper fitting, training on usage, and follow-up support for maintenance.
