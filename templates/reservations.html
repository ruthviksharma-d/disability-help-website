Educational Reservations

Higher Education (5% Reservation)

Reserved seats in colleges and universities for persons with benchmark disabilities

Covered Institutions:

  • Central Universities
  • State Universities
  • IITs, IIMs, NITs
  • Medical and Engineering Colleges
  • Teacher Training Institutes

Disability Categories:

Blindness Low Vision Hearing Impairment Locomotor Disability Cerebral Palsy Autism Intellectual Disability

School Education

Inclusive education policies ensuring access to quality education

  • Free education up to 18 years
  • Reasonable accommodation in examinations
  • Additional time allowances
  • Accessible infrastructure

Employment Reservations

Government Jobs (4% Reservation)

Reserved positions in central and state government departments

Central Government:

  • • Group A: 4%
  • • Group B: 4%
  • • Group C: 4%
  • • Group D: 4%

State Government:

  • • Class I: 4%
  • • Class II: 4%
  • • Class III: 4%
  • • Class IV: 4%

Sub-quotas within 4%:

Visual Impairment: 1%
Hearing Impairment: 1%
Locomotor Disability: 1%
Multiple Disabilities: 1%

Private Sector

Incentives and guidelines for private employers

  • Tax benefits for hiring PWDs
  • Accessibility compliance requirements
  • Equal opportunity policies

State-wise Reservation Policies

Maharashtra

  • Education: 5% in colleges
  • Employment: 4% in state jobs
  • Special: Additional quota in professional courses

Karnataka

  • Education: 5% + super-numerary seats
  • Employment: 4% in all categories
  • Special: Free bus passes

Tamil Nadu

  • Education: 5% horizontal reservation
  • Employment: 4% across all services
  • Special: Land allotment preferences

How to Apply for Reservations

1

Obtain Disability Certificate

Get certified by a competent medical authority with at least 40% disability

2

Apply for UDID Card

Register for Unique Disability Identity Card for unified access to benefits

3

Submit Applications

Apply for educational institutions or jobs mentioning disability category

4

Provide Documentation

Submit required certificates and documents during application process
