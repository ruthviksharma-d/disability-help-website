Emergency Preparedness Tips

Before an Emergency

  • Keep emergency contacts handy
    Store important numbers in your phone and keep a written copy
  • Medical information card
    Carry details about your disability, medications, and emergency contacts
  • Emergency kit
    Keep essential medications, batteries for devices, and backup equipment
  • Know your evacuation plan
    Identify accessible exits and safe meeting points

During an Emergency

  • Stay calm and assess the situation
    Take a moment to understand what's happening and your options
  • Call for help immediately
    Dial 112 or the appropriate emergency number
  • Communicate your disability
    Inform responders about your specific needs and assistive devices
  • Follow instructions from authorities
    Ask for clarification if instructions are not accessible to you
